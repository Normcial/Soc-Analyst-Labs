# Intro to SOC

## What is a SOC?
A Security Operations Center (SOC) is a centralized team that monitors,
detects, and responds to cybersecurity threats and incidents within
an organization.

## What is the Blue Team responsible for?
The Blue Team is responsible for defending systems and networks by
monitoring activity, identifying threats, and responding to security
incidents.

## What does a Tier 1 SOC analyst do?
A Tier 1 SOC analyst monitors security alerts, performs initial
investigation, filters out false positives, and escalates confirmed
incidents to higher-tier analysts.
