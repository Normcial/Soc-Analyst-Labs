# Splunk Alert Triage

## What triggered the alert?
The alert was triggered based on suspicious activity detected in log data,
indicating potential security issues that required investigation.

## What logs were reviewed?
Relevant logs were reviewed in Splunk to understand the source,
frequency, and context of the alert.

## How was severity determined?
Severity was determined based on the impact, affected systems,
and whether the activity appeared malicious or benign.

## Investigation outcome
The investigation identified whether the alert was a false positive
or required escalation for deeper analysis.

## Escalation decision
Based on the findings, the alert was escalated to a higher tier
for further investigation.
